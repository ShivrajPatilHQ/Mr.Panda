What he does

Small footprint.
Serious output.

Click him and his laptop opens — a focused workspace with two desks, built for founders doing outreach.

Research with sources

Finds and vets investors, companies, and markets using live Google Search — and cites where every fact came from. He never invents a name, an email, or a number.

Ready-to-send writing

Ask for a cold email or a LinkedIn message and get a finished draft — subject line, body, sign-off. Copy it, or let him paste it directly into the field you were typing in.

Humanize any text

Select AI-sounding text in any app, pick a voice — Subtle, Human, CEO, Founder, or your own — and he rewrites it and pastes it back over your selection.

Collect & export

Save contacts from research replies into a clean, deduplicated list — names, firms, emails, links — and export everything to CSV in one click.

Quietly alive

He strolls your screen and sits in a corner with bamboo while you work. When you need him, he's one click away. When you don't, he stays out of the way.

Private by design

No account. No sign-up. Runs on a hosted free tier, or bring your own Gemini key — stored only on your Mac, sent only to Google.

Free to start.

Five messages a day on the house — no key, no signup. Bring your own Gemini key for unlimited use, free forever.
