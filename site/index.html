One panda. Two desks.

Click him — his laptop opens. Research on one tab, Writing on the other.

🔎

Research desk

Finds and vets investors, companies, and markets with live Google Search — and cites where every fact came from. No invented emails, no made-up numbers.

✍️

Writing desk

Ask for a cold email or LinkedIn DM and get a ready-to-send draft — subject line, body, sign-off. Copy it, or let him paste it straight into the box you were typing in.

✨

Humanize

Select AI-ish text in any app, pick a voice — Subtle, Human, CEO, Founder, or your own — and he rewrites it and pastes it back over your selection. Em-dashes eliminated.

📌

Collect → CSV

Hit Save under a research reply and he extracts every contact into a deduped list — names, firms, emails, links — exportable to CSV in one click.

🐾
