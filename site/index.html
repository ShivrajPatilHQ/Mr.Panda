What he does

Small footprint. Serious output.

Click him and his laptop opens — two focused desks, built for founders doing outreach.

research

Answers with receipts

Finds and vets investors, companies, and markets using live Google Search — and cites where every fact came from. He never invents a name, an email, or a number.

write

Ready-to-send drafts

Ask for a cold email or a LinkedIn message and get a finished draft — subject, body, sign-off. Copy it, or let him paste it straight into the field you were typing in.

humanize

De-AI any text

Select text anywhere, pick a voice — Subtle, Human, CEO, Founder, or describe your own — and he rewrites it over your selection, in place.

collect

Contacts → CSV

Save contacts from research replies into a clean, deduplicated list — names, firms, emails, links — and export the lot to CSV in one click.

companion

Quietly alive

He strolls your screen and sits in a corner with bamboo while you work. One click away when you need him, out of the way when you don't.

private

No account. Ever.

No sign-up, no tracking dashboard, no email capture. A free hosted tier out of the box, or bring your own Gemini key — stored only on your Mac.
